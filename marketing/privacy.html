SEO Vision Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2025

1. Data Collection

SEO Vision does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal user information
  • Website content you analyze
  • Browsing history
  • Any other sensitive data

2. How We Process Information

All analysis occurs locally in your browser:

  • Page content is processed temporarily during analysis
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Results are never stored persistently

3. Permissions Explanation

The activeTab permission allows:

  • Reading the current webpage's DOM for SEO analysis
  • Functionality only when you click the extension icon

4. Changes to This Policy

Policy updates will be posted on this page with revised "Last Updated" dates.
